Help with ShiftSum

Support

For questions, troubleshooting, or feedback, email ShiftSum support.

Contact

shiftsum.app.support@gmail.com

Please include only the information you are comfortable sharing. ShiftSum support will never ask for your Apple Account password, verification codes, banking information, or complete shift history.

Helpful details

If relevant, you can mention your iPhone model, iOS version, the screen where the issue occurred, and the steps that led to it. Avoid including wages, employer details, or exported CSV files unless they are genuinely necessary and you choose to share them.
